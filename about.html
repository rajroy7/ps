About the Developer

Project Skirk - A Fan Database Built with Passion

Why Project Skirk?

Project Skirk was created in my free time to fill a void left by beloved community resources like Homdgcat and Hakush.in. As a passionate Genshin Impact player and developer, I realized there was a need for a comprehensive, well-organized fan database that consolidates all the essential information about characters, weapons, artifacts, and game content.

This website is my way of giving back to the community and creating a resource that helps players navigate the complex world of Genshin Impact with ease and efficiency.

What's Included

  • Comprehensive character guides and builds
  • Complete weapon database and rankings
  • Artifact set optimization strategies
  • Enemy and creature information
  • Spiral Abyss challenges
  • Event guides and limited-time content
  • TCG and game meta insights
  • And much more to come!
